Calibration & Accuracy

For the most precise results, calibrate your phone's compass by moving it in a smooth figure-8 motion in the air.

A slight difference of a few degrees in Qibla direction is still within acceptable tolerance according to many scholars and astronomers. Do not worry about very small deviations.
